Once an audit finishes, you can take the report with you.

## Export to PDF

Use the export action in the Studio to download a formatted PDF. The PDF includes:

- The overall diagnosis and score breakdown
- Priority fixes
- The source-verification summary and a starter bibliography
- Your submitted draft

The PDF is formatted for clean reading and printing, which is useful for handing feedback to a coach or teacher.

## Save to Past Work

Signed-in users can save audits to the workspace and return to them later from the **Past Work** page. This keeps your drafts and their reports together so you can track revisions over time.

<Tip>
Re-run an audit after you revise. Comparing the new score breakdown to the old one shows whether your fixes actually strengthened the argument.
</Tip>
