description: "A focused read on a personal statement or supplemental essay."
---

College essays are personal and narrative, so use **Essay** mode rather than Argument mode. Fracture reads for clarity, structure, and voice instead of debate-style attacks.

## What it checks

- Whether your central idea or throughline is clear
- Opening hook and whether it earns the reader's attention
- Paragraph flow and whether each section advances the story
- Sentence clarity, tone, and word choice
- Your single strongest and weakest sentence, with a complete rewrite for the weakest

## Tips for college essays

- Choose **Essay** mode and **Medium** depth for a balanced read.
- Use the strongest and weakest sentence callouts to decide what to protect and what to cut.
- Ask Fracture Chat follow-up questions about tone or a specific paragraph.

<Warning>
Fracture will not invent achievements, details, or experiences. Keep every rewrite true to your own story.
</Warning>
