---
title: "Depth Levels"
description: "Choose how deep the Fracture Engine reads."
---

Depth controls how much the engine produces and who the report is written for. Pick the level that matches how much revision you are ready to do.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Surface">
    A fast pass focused on the two or three things that matter most. Best for a quick check before submitting.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Medium">
    The default. A full audit with claim analysis, assumptions, counterarguments, a source check, and a complete revision path.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Extreme">
    The deepest read. Eight or more priority fixes, a full dependency graph, an expanded attack tree, and extra argument angles to research.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How depth changes the report

| | Surface | Medium | Extreme |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Priority fixes | ~3 | ~5 | 8+ |
| Counterarguments | Few | Several | Many |
| Dependency graph | Minimal | Standard | Detailed |
| Audience | Casual writer | Serious student or debater | Researcher or competitor |

<Tip>
Start at Medium. Drop to Surface for a fast pre-submission check, or move to Extreme when you are preparing for a high-stakes round or final submission.
</Tip>
