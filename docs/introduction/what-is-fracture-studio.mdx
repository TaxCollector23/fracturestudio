---
title: "What is Fracture Studio?"
description: "An argument auditing tool that turns any draft into a clear repair plan."
---

Fracture Studio is an argument auditing tool for anyone who writes to persuade. You paste in your writing — a speech, debate case, Model UN position paper, college essay, research paper, or anything else — and the **Fracture Engine** returns a full analysis in under a minute.

Instead of broad reassurance, Fracture reads your argument the way a coach, teacher, judge, or opponent would. It identifies what you are actually trying to prove, where the reasoning holds, and where it breaks first.

## What you get back

- An overall score with a breakdown across the parts of an argument that decide whether it survives.
- A ranked list of priority fixes, ordered by how much each one improves the piece.
- The single point your argument depends on most, and what happens if it is challenged.
- Hidden assumptions, likely counterarguments, and the questions an opponent would ask.
- A source check that flags which factual claims have support and which still need a citation.

## How it works at a glance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Paste your draft">
    Drop your writing into the Studio and pick a mode and depth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The Fracture Engine reads it">
    The engine maps your claims, warrants, evidence, and impacts, then pressure-tests each one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read your report">
    You get a structured report you can act on immediately, plus follow-up tools like Fracture Chat and a rebuttal builder.
  </Step>
</Steps>
