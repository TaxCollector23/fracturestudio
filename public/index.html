Full Feature Breakdown

Eight lenses.
One complete audit.

Every analysis generates a structured JSON report across eight analytical dimensions. No fluff. No filler. Everything anchored to your text.

4-Dimensional Scoring

A 100-point composite score broken into Argument Strength, Assumption Audit, Logic, and Rhetoric — each out of 25. Know exactly where you're strong and where you're bleeding points.

Claim-by-Claim Autopsy

Every body claim is rated STRONG, MODERATE, or WEAK. Each gets a named logical flaw, a precise diagnosis, the exact exploit a skilled opponent would use, and a concrete fix.

Hidden Assumption Audit

Surfaces every premise you're assuming but not defending. Each assumption is rated by load-bearing weight — what breaks if it's false, and how to defend it when challenged.

Logical Fallacy Detection

Named fallacies only. No vague "this could be better." Each fallacy is identified by exact name, quoted verbatim, explained in context, and given a complete rewrite.

Steelmanned Counter-Arguments

We build the single strongest version of the opposing argument — not a strawman. Then we show which specific claims it defeats and how to preempt it before anyone uses it against you.

Rhetorical Analysis

Evaluates opening hook, paragraph flow, and identifies your single strongest and weakest sentence verbatim. The weakest sentence includes a complete rewrite — not instructions, a replacement.

Rewrite Suggestions

Full sentence rewrites for your most impactful improvement opportunities. Not "be more specific" — we write the better version for you to adopt, adapt, or learn from.

Export, Share & Save

Export your full analysis as JSON. Generate a shareable link with the full report encoded. Save essays locally for later. Your analysis, your way.
