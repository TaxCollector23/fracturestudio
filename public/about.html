About Fracture Studio

Professional argument feedback should not be locked behind private coaching.

Fracture Studio is built for students, debaters, speakers, and writers who need more than a grammar check. It helps users see the claims, warrants, assumptions, counterarguments, and revision priorities inside their own work.

Analyze a Draft Read the Method
Mission

Speech and Debate feedback can be expensive, slow, and unevenly available. Fracture Studio gives more students a serious way to test an argument before it reaches a teacher, judge, opponent, or audience.

What We Build For

The product is designed around exactness: quote the problem, explain why it matters, show what breaks, and recommend the smallest high-impact repair.

What We Avoid

Fracture does not try to flatter a draft or overwhelm the user with academic labels. The goal is readable, usable feedback that still respects serious argument theory.

Founders

Built around access, clarity, and useful pressure.

Fracture Studio was created by student builders who wanted argument coaching to feel more available and more concrete. The founders are intentionally keeping the product focused on the work a writer can actually do next: improve the thesis, repair the warrant, answer the strongest objection, and understand what a judge or reader may question.

Founder background details will be added here as the team publishes them. For now, this page keeps the focus on the product mission rather than inventing personal details.
