---
title: "Who it is for"
description: "Students, debaters, and serious writers who want specific, actionable feedback."
---

Fracture Studio is built for people who are graded, judged, or challenged on what they write and say.

## Debaters

Test a case before a round. Find the load-bearing claim, the assumptions an opponent will attack, and the crossfire questions you should be ready to answer. The rebuttal builder turns your own draft into opponent preparation.

## Speech and presentation writers

Grade a piece the way an audience hears it once — hook, pacing, signposting, and the lines a listener will remember or lose. Speech mode prioritizes clarity and delivery over debate-style attacks.

## Students and essay writers

Sharpen a thesis, fix paragraph flow, integrate evidence, and clean up academic language. Essay mode reads like a strong English teacher rather than a debate judge.

## Model UN delegates

Pressure-test a position paper against the questions other delegates and the chair will raise, and find where your policy needs a clearer warrant or a stronger source.

## Researchers and applicants

Check a research paper's claims and citations, or get a focused read on a college essay before you submit it.

<Note>
Fracture is especially useful for students who may not have regular access to private coaching. It gives a serious first-pass review that is easy to understand and act on.
</Note>
