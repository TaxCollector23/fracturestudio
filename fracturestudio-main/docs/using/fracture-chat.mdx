---
title: "Using Fracture Chat"
description: "Ask follow-up questions about your draft and report."
---

Fracture Chat is a coaching conversation that already knows your current draft and your audit report. Use it to go deeper on anything the report surfaced.

## What it is good for

- Asking about a specific pressure point or quoted passage
- Getting a complete replacement sentence for a weak line
- Working through the next one to three revision moves
- Following up without re-pasting your draft

## How it behaves

- It builds on earlier turns in the same session instead of restarting each time.
- It writes in plain text and gives a concise answer first, expanding only when you ask.
- It never invents evidence, statistics, or sources. When a fact is needed, it marks the gap and names the kind of evidence to look for.

<Tip>
Select a pressure point in the report before asking, and Fracture Chat will focus its answer on that exact passage.
</Tip>
