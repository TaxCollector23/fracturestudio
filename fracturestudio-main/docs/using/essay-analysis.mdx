title: "Essay Analysis"
description: "Academic and analytical writing graded like a strong English teacher would."
---

Essay mode reads your writing the way a strong English teacher or academic reader would. The writing can be analytical, explanatory, narrative, persuasive, or literary — Fracture does not force every essay into a debate-case structure.

## What it prioritizes

- Thesis or central idea, and whether it is clear and arguable
- Paragraph structure and whether each paragraph does a clear job
- Transitions and overall flow
- Evidence integration
- Grammar, sentence clarity, tone, and word choice
- A conclusion that earns its place

## What you get

Essay-specific repairs: grammar and syntax fixes, paragraph reordering, transition improvements, a sharper thesis, a cleaner evidence sandwich, and more natural academic language. Critical reasoning is still applied when the essay makes claims, but the report does not over-index on opponent attacks unless the piece is explicitly argumentative.
